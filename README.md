# Warehouse-Performance-Summary- (Real Data) 
This project analyses operational performance across the Voltex warehouse from June to December 2025, covering picking, replenishment, inbound transactions, and cycle count accuracy.
The dataset includes warehouse‑level metrics, team performance, and SKU‑level insights.

Objective
To provide end-to-end visibility of warehouse performance across:

- Picking
- Replenishment
- Inbound transactions
- Inventory accuracy (cycle counts)

The dashboard supports both executive oversight and operational decision-making.

- Key Features
- KPI cards for volume and loss tracking
- Performance by warehouse and team member
- Monthly trend analysis (line & bar charts)
- Top-N item analysis with drill-through to full item lists
- Combined count and % contribution views

Insights Delivered
- Identified peak operational months and demand slowdowns
- Highlighted reliance on a small group of pickers and SKUs
- Exposed inventory accuracy risks by warehouse and item
- Demonstrated alignment (and lag) between picking and replenishment

Business Value
- Enables proactive labour and replenishment planning
- Reduces operational risk through better visibility
- Supports inventory accuracy improvements
- Scales easily with additional time periods or KPIs
